Overview

You may be wondering. What is Computer Hardware? Well you've come to the right place.
Computer Hardware are the physical components of a computer system that you can see and touch.

Basically, the body of the computer that makes it work!

In this small guide, we'll cover the basics and examples of computer hardware,
as well as where you can find some pieces of equiptment for yourself!



Let's begin with the basics!
Hardware can be broken down into a few different categories.

  • Internal Hardware
  • External Hardware
  • Input Devices
  • Output Devices
